About This Project

Clippings About Legal

This website preserves and shares primary-source newspaper clippings related to the 1985–1987 investigation into the death of Loretta Lynn Willoughby in Estill County, Kentucky. It aims to provide public access and context for researchers, journalists, and the community.

Each entry includes a scan (crop) from the original newspaper page, a transcript (OCR), and a link to the full page PDF for verification. Where possible, we include publication details and dates.

How it’s organized

  • Clippings are grouped by source (Estill County Tribune; Citizen Voice & Times) and issue.
  • The search bar looks through source names and excerpts.
  • Use the source filter to narrow results.
